1. Big picture — booking is finance

1. Visión general — booking es finance

Every place that “books” a child eventually creates money work: an INV-P invoice in Portal (for the family) and, when paid, a matching record in Xero (for the books).

Todo sitio donde se “reserva” a un niño acaba en trabajo de dinero: una factura INV-P en Portal (para la familia) y, al pagar, el registro en Xero (contabilidad).

3. Re-enrolments (existing families)

3. Re-enrolments (familias existentes)

Parent pays their chosen plan for the next year / term. Portal creates the instalment invoices automatically on first submit (parent-pays families only — not LA office / funder invoice).

El padre elige el plan del próximo año / término. Portal crea las facturas a plazos solos en el primer envío (solo familias que pagan ellos — no LA / funder).

Steps

Pasos

  1. Parent opens hub → Re-enrolment → chooses plan → submits.
  2. Portal creates INV-P rows (e.g. Autumn / Spring / Summer instalments) already shared to Invoices.
  3. Parent pays each due invoice (card, GC, bank, or credit).
  4. Office watches Finance → Re-enrolments: chase unpaid, confirm bank, check Xero.
  1. Padre abre hub → Re-enrolment → elige plan → envía.
  2. Portal crea filas INV-P (p. ej. Autumn / Spring / Summer) ya compartidas en Invoices.
  3. El padre paga cada factura cuando toca (tarjeta, GC, banco o crédito).
  4. Oficina mira Finance → Re-enrolments: cobra impagos, confirma banco, revisa Xero.
Example — Maya, Autumn bank transfer
1) Mum submits re-enrolment with Bank Transfer.
2) INV-P-0142 appears for Autumn (£1,200), draft PDF ready.
3) Mum transfers to Tide using reference Maya (participant name), then taps I've paid by bank transfer.
4) Office filter Pending confirmation → open Match Tide bank CSV → Upload & score → Confirm.
5) Invoice is PAID in Portal; Xero ACCREC + Payment created; green tick still OK’d in Xero bank feed if needed.
Ejemplo — Maya, Autumn por transferencia
1) La madre envía el re-enrolment con Bank Transfer.
2) Aparece INV-P-0142 de Autumn (£1,200), PDF draft listo.
3) Transfiere a Tide con referencia Maya (nombre del participante) y pulsa I've paid by bank transfer.
4) Oficina filtra Pending confirmation → Match Tide bank CSV → Upload & score → Confirm.
5) Factura PAID en Portal; ACCREC + Payment en Xero; el tick verde se OK’ea en el bank feed de Xero si hace falta.
Resubmits: changing the plan later does not auto-create duplicate invoices. Adjust amounts / create new rows in Finance.
Reenvíos: cambiar el plan después no duplica facturas solos. Ajusta importes / crea filas nuevas en Finance.

4. New clients & crash summer booking

4. Clientes nuevos y booking crash summer

Public booking page (e.g. /parent/crash-summer). Parent reserves a place and must pay in full within ~2 hours or the hold releases.

Página pública de booking (p. ej. /parent/crash-summer). El padre reserva plaza y debe pagar entero en ~2 horas o se libera el hold.

Steps

Pasos

  1. Parent completes booking → Reserve & pay in full.
  2. Portal creates a full-amount INV-P + PDF (+ email). Crash also best-effort pushes ACCREC to Xero early.
  3. Parent pays on the booking page (Tide / Card) or via hub Invoices.
  4. When paid, the crash place is confirmed. Same office tools if bank transfer was used.
  1. Padre completa booking → Reserve & pay in full.
  2. Portal crea INV-P de importe completo + PDF (+ email). Crash también intenta ACCREC en Xero pronto.
  3. Paga en la página de booking (Tide / tarjeta) o en hub Invoices.
  4. Al pagar, se confirma la plaza crash. Mismas herramientas de oficina si fue transferencia.
Example — new family, SwimFarm crash Week 1
1) Dad books Wed–Fri crash online.
2) INV-P-0201 created for £450; email with PDF; 2-hour hold starts.
3) He pays Card / Apple Pay → Stripe webhook marks PAID → place confirmed.
4) Office sees the row under Finance → Re-enrolments / Payments (Summer crash). No Tide step needed.
Ejemplo — familia nueva, crash SwimFarm Week 1
1) El padre reserva crash mié–vie online.
2) Se crea INV-P-0201 de £450; email con PDF; hold de 2 horas.
3) Paga Card / Apple Pay → webhook Stripe marca PAID → plaza confirmada.
4) Oficina ve la fila en Finance → Re-enrolments / Payments (Summer crash). No hace falta Tide.

7. Suggested office daily routine

7. Rutina diaria sugerida de oficina

  1. Open Finance → Re-enrolments → filter Pending confirmation.
  2. Export Tide inbound CSV → Match Tide bank CSV → Upload & score → Confirm strong matches; review medium.
  3. Filter Ready unpaid / Buffer low → chase parents (Remind / Soft hold if needed).
  4. Filter Paid not in Xero → Push paid to Xero for any leftovers / failures.
  5. In Xero bank feed, OK suggested matches (or leave to JAX if enabled).
  1. Abre Finance → Re-enrolments → filtro Pending confirmation.
  2. Exporta CSV de Tide → Match Tide bank CSV → Upload & score → Confirm strong; revisa medium.
  3. Filtro Ready unpaid / Buffer low → cobra a padres (Remind / Soft hold si hace falta).
  4. Filtro Paid not in Xero → Push paid to Xero para restos / fallos.
  5. En el bank feed de Xero, OK a los matches sugeridos (o deja JAX si está activo).

8. Xero & Tide — what each button does

8. Xero y Tide — qué hace cada botón

ActionAcción Why it existsPara qué existe
Push paid to Xero Push paid to Xero Backlog / retries: create ACCREC + Payment for paid Portal INV-Ps. Normal pay paths usually auto-do this now. Cola / reintentos: crea ACCREC + Payment para INV-P pagados. Los pagos normales ya suelen hacerlo solos.
Match Tide bank CSV Match Tide bank CSV Bank has no webhook. Links Tide money → Portal paid (+ Xero books). Uses Tide date on the Payment. El banco no tiene webhook. Enlaza dinero Tide → Portal paid (+ libros Xero). Usa la fecha Tide en el Payment.
Confirm paid / Mark paid Confirm paid / Mark paid Manual when you know money arrived (cash, odd ref, skip CSV). Manual cuando sabes que llegó el dinero (efectivo, ref rara, sin CSV).
Export to Xero CSV Export to Xero CSV Fallback if API push fails. Plan B si falla el push por API.
Green reconcile tick Tick verde de reconcile Only in Xero. Portal cannot close bank-feed lines by API. Solo en Xero. Portal no puede cerrar líneas del bank feed por API.
