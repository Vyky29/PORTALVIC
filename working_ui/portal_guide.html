Portal guide

Everything you need to use the clubSENsational portal on your phone.

Getting started on your phone

The Portal has been designed to be used on your phone during and after your sessions.

As a priority, please:

  1. Log into the Portal using the link provided.
  2. Complete the first section of this guide.
  3. Install the App on your mobile device following the instructions below.

When you install the app, the home-screen icon is labelled CS Portal automatically.

If you did not save your password when logging in through the web page, the App may not recognise your account when you first log in. If this happens, simply log out and log back into the App.

Once the App has been installed, please go to the Quick Menu, scroll down to Settings, and activate Notifications/Alerts.

Please also note:

  • Microphone access is optional. You only need it if you prefer to give feedback by speaking instead of typing.
  • Location access appears in Settings only for staff on Bespoke Programme or Day Centre, and is used only during the hours of that service.

Finally, please keep Zoho Cliq installed for now. You may delete the Zoho People App if you wish, as you will be able to find payslips and company documents through the Portal under My Documents.

Going forward, you should only need two company Apps on your phone:

  1. CS Portal (operations)
  2. Zoho Cliq (chat)

Install it like an app

Add the portal to your home screen — it installs as CS Portal with the clubSENsational icon, full-screen, no browser bar.

iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open the portal in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow).
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen, then Add.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open the portal in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right).
  3. Choose Install app / Add to Home screen.

Sign in once and the app remembers you. If you ever get signed out, just open it again and log in.

CS Portal

Once added, the portal sits on your home screen with the club icon and opens full-screen — just like any other app.

What the colours mean

The portal uses colour so you can tell at a glance what needs your attention. Same colours everywhere — session cards, pills and the logo.

Session cards & reviews

On a busy day you may see several participants in one list — each card keeps its own colour until you review that session.

Monday at a glance — four sessions on the same day. Orange cards still need feedback; green with Absent means you already closed that session.

Blue — scheduledAn upcoming session. Nothing to do yet.
Orange — action neededFeedback is still pending for this session. Tap it to submit.
Green — doneFeedback submitted (or absence recorded). All good.
Red — incident / urgentAn incident was reported, or something needs sorting now.
Purple — schedule changeA cancellation or roster change affecting this slot.

The logo halo (the dots around the logo)

When something is waiting for you, the dots circling the logo change colour:

Announcement to sign
New chat message
Feedback pending
Several at once

Orange — feedback still outstanding. The gold halo on the logo matches the reminder count.

Green — reviewed and closed. Here the session is marked Absent (participant did not attend). Feedback submitted counts as green too.

Status pills

Little rounded tags on cards and lists. They tell you the state of a session or task:

Done Pending Late Absent Incident Scheduled
  • Done — feedback/report submitted.
  • Pending — still needs your feedback.
  • Late — submitted (or being submitted) after the deadline.
  • Absent — the participant didn’t attend.
  • Incident — an incident report is attached.

My participants

Open My participants from the bottom bar to see everyone on your roster. Switch between My participants and All participants, then tap a child to open their card — quick actions pre-fill the participant for Feedback, Absence, Incident, or Cancellation.

Your roster under My participants. Tap a name to open their session card and quick actions.

All participants shows everyone at the club in a grid — useful when you need to look someone up outside your own roster.

Each participant card shows their session time and quick actions. Use Feedback here when you already know which child the form is for.

Tap General Info for goals, medical notes, and how the participant communicates — handy before a home or community session.

The Quick menu

Everything you submit lives here. Open it from your dashboard — scroll for more sections (Finance, Documents, Training).

  • Session feedback — how the session went (engagement, regulation, independence, notes).
  • Incident report — anything that needs recording for safeguarding/safety.
  • Cancellation — when a session can’t go ahead.
  • Expenses — submit your expenses; a PDF is saved to your documents.
  • Timesheet — log your hours for the month.
  • Venue review — opening/closing checks for your venue.
  • Pickup / handover — record who collected a participant.
  • Update my profile — confirm your personal & emergency details.

The Quick menu groups every form in one place — timesheets and expenses under Finance, PDFs under My documents, and training under Training. Tiles you don’t need simply won’t show for your role.

Further down: Compliance (policies, risk assessments, safeguarding) and Settings — including this Guide, alerts/permissions, and Logout.

Tip: open a form from the session card itself when you can — it pre-fills the participant, date and time for you.

Expenses

Submit receipts and claims from Quick menu → Expenses under Finance. A PDF is saved to My documents when you submit.

  • Add each line: Description, Amount (GBP), and Upload receipt photo.
  • Use + Add row for multiple items; check the Total before submitting.

Expense claim — one row per receipt. Month and employee are pre-filled; attach a photo for each line where you have one.

Timesheet

Open from Quick menu → Timesheets under Finance. Submit by the 24th of the month so hours land in that month’s pay.

  • Green rows — session feedback complete; included in ready to pay now.
  • Orange rows — feedback still pending; shown but excluded until you complete feedback.
  • Submitting on the 25th or later moves the timesheet to the next month and may add a penalty.

Green = paid when feedback is done. Orange = waiting on feedback — complete the session feedback from your dashboard to turn the row green.

Venue review

Record opening or closing checks for your venue. Open from Quick menu → Venue review.

  • Confirm venue, date, and time (pre-filled where possible).
  • Answer Any issues, damages or incidents to report? — No or Yes.
  • Tap Submit when done.

Venue review — quick check at open or close. Choose No when all is fine, or Yes if something needs reporting.

Drop off & pick up

Record when a participant arrives and when they are collected. Open from Quick menu → Drop Off & Pick Up.

  • Type a name to open today’s handover for someone on your roster.
  • Tap New for a participant not listed yet.
  • Open handovers for today appear below the search box.

Drop off & pick up — search or tap New to start a handover for today.

An open handover shows Drop Off details first — participant, date, time, and how they seemed on arrival. Complete Pick Up when they are collected.

Alerts, location & microphone

Turn on notifications once on each device. Open from Quick menu → Alerts, location & mic under Settings.

  • Notifications — announcements and roster changes (use Send test to check).
  • Location — only shown in Settings for Bespoke Programme and Day Centre staff, during your session window (from 15 minutes before until 30 minutes after).
  • Microphone — optional; only if you want to give session feedback by voice instead of typing.

Enable notifications here. Location only applies to Bespoke Programme and Day Centre sessions; microphone is optional for voice feedback.

Chats

Message the office from Alerts/Notifications → Internal chat, or open an existing thread when the logo halo is blue.

  • A blue halo on the logo means you have a new message.
  • You can send text or a voice note (mic next to the message box).
  • Chats are private between you and the person/office you’re messaging.

Internal chat — messages with the office. Type below or tap the mic for a voice note, then Send.

Announcements & reminders

Tap the club logo on your dashboard to open Alerts/Notifications — the guide, internal chat, signed announcements, and reminders in one place.

  • Under Reminders, Outstanding feedbacks x1 (orange) opens the session still waiting for feedback.
  • Tap Term on the dashboard to see which days in the term still need feedback (red on the calendar).
  • When the office posts an announcement, the logo halo turns red and a banner appears.
  • Read announcements and tap Sign / confirm so the office knows you’ve seen them.
  • After you sign, announcements move to the Signed announcements log with date and time.
  • Employment contracts to sign also appear here; the signed PDF is saved under My Documents.

Tap the logo to open Alerts/Notifications. The bright orange Outstanding feedbacks x1 tile under Reminders takes you straight to the pending session.

Open Term from your dashboard to see the whole term at a glance. Days in red still have session feedback outstanding; tap a day to jump to that session.

Once signed, announcements appear in the Signed announcements log. When something new is waiting, the logo halo turns red and you’ll see a Sign / confirm banner first.
