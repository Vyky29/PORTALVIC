Admin portal guide

How the operations admin app is organised, what each area does today, and how it connects to staff and CEO portals.

What this app is

Admin dashboard (admin_dashboard.html) is the control room for ops, managers, and CEOs. It runs in the browser (desktop or tablet) on the same Vercel origin as staff and lead dashboards.

  • Left sidebar — grouped navigation (Operator, Services & participants, HR, Documents, Finance, Settings).
  • Top bar — global search, date, alerts, profile (opens Settings).
  • Main workspace — the page you picked from the menu; content swaps without leaving the app.
  • Supabase — live data (roster rows, feedback, documents, DMs, timetable overrides) after you sign in with your club account.

Not the same as the staff app. Instructors use Staff dashboard for their own sessions and feedback. Admin sees everyone’s data, scheduling tools, and office workflows. CEOs can switch portals from Settings (Staff / Lead / Admin).

Operator & day operations

Dashboard and Operator are your morning control screens: what needs a human today (late feedback, absences, covers, comms).

  • Day operations hub — cards for internal chat, CEO chat (if you are Victor/Raúl/Javi), staff map, and cross-links into sessions.
  • Operations admin — deeper ops board (incidents, suggestions, mock KPIs where demo data is still wired).
  • Portal activity — who opened which portal and when (audit-style visit log).
  • Staff live map — last known location ping from staff devices (when enabled).
See KPI / alert → Open Sessions hub or participant → Fix roster / feedback / comms

Participants directory

Search any child, open their participant workspace (booking, medical, assessments, session feedback history, documents). This is the admin view of one family — not “my clients” on the staff phone.

Schedule & covers · Edit term slot

Change who teaches a recurring slot, mark covers, and sync with portal_roster_rows in Supabase. Needs admin or CEO role for saves (RLS).

Spreadsheet reference

Mirror of the Excel rosters: group sessions grid and staff hours (from 1 Jun 2026). Staff-hours cells can be edited and saved — overrides flow to dashboards via portal_staff_timetable_cells.

Reviews, reports, orders, comms

  • Reviews / reports — oversight of submitted session feedback and lead reports.
  • Orders & absents/refunds — billing-related queues (with demo blocks where noted).
  • Communications ops — outbound comms tied to operations (templates vary by build).

Staff & HR · Documents · Finance

Staff & HR

  • Roster, shifts, contracts embed.
  • Onboarding pipeline and interviews list.
  • Quick links to Zoho-style HR screens where integrated.

Documents (Supabase)

  • Payslips — upload for staff; workers see theirs under My documents.
  • Timesheets, expenses, PINs, certificates, passports, checklists, first aid.

Finance (Xero)

Re-enrolment payments and invoices views — aligned with club billing spreadsheets where live data is connected.

Settings & other portals

Settings & dashboards (sidebar) holds preferences, activity log, alerts inbox, and links to:

  • Staff dashboard — instructor day view (your own sessions only).
  • Lead dashboard — programme lead tools.
  • CEO dashboard — executive summaries.
  • Operations admin — this app.

CEO / exec users: opening Staff dashboard shows your identity only — not another instructor’s participant list. If you have no pool sessions, My participants stays empty; use Admin for company-wide views.

Staff-facing help lives in Portal guide (mobile-first, colours, feedback, announcements).

Tips & permissions

  • Search — participants and staff from the top bar; some views add their own filters.
  • Alerts bell — portal notices; may overlap with day-ops reminders.
  • Saving roster / schedule — requires app_role admin or ceo in Supabase (or known username overrides).
  • Demo labels — some KPI cards still say “demo”; live session rows come from imported roster + Supabase merges.
  • Logout — Settings or sidebar “Log out”; clears Supabase session on this origin.
