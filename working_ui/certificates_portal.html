Certificates

Training certificates and qualification records for staff, in one place.

clubSENsational · staff portal

What will be here

A dedicated hub for formal certificates and evidence that sit outside day-to-day portal PDFs: pool qualifications, first aid, safeguarding completions (e.g. after you download a certificate from external training such as NSPCC My Learning), and other accredited courses.

Relationship to My Documents

My Documents continues to hold PDFs that the portal generates for you (for example timesheets, expenses, and swimming term reviews). Certificates will focus on files you upload or that we attach as official qualification records, so staff can find “what I earned elsewhere” without mixing it up with payroll or review PDFs.

Planned features

  • List of your certificate files with title, type, and date.
  • Secure download of your own documents.
  • Optional reminders for expiry or renewal where we store an expiry date.
  • Same sign-in as the rest of the staff portal.

What you can do today

Use Safeguarding in the quick menu to open NSPCC My Learning and complete mandatory training. When Certificates goes live, you will be able to file the downloaded certificate here.
