Rating Scale

Scale items use 1-3. Yes/No items score as Yes=3 and No=1.

1
LimitedPractice is limited and needs significant improvement.
2
InconsistentSome elements are in place but not reliably sustained.
3
StrongPractice is consistently strong and purposeful.
AProfessionalism & Safety
1. Punctuality and readinessArrives on time, prepared to start, with the session ready and no unnecessary delays.
2. Dress and presentationAppropriate uniform or clothing, clean, suitable for the activity and environment, professional appearance.
3. Professional communication and conductRespectful tone, appropriate language, clear and positive interaction with participants, families and staff.
4. Safety, safeguarding and proceduresAwareness of safety, safeguarding, risk management, and adherence to venue and internal procedures.
BEngagement & Session Delivery
1. Builds positive connection and rapportWarm approach, trust, responsiveness, participant feels comfortable and supported.
2. Promotes and sustains engagementEncourages involvement, offers opportunities and choices, avoids passive time, adapts when engagement drops, and keeps the participant meaningfully involved throughout the session.
3. Uses resources to support engagementEffective use of equipment, visuals and space to maintain interest, structure and clarity.
4. Session structure and flowClear beginning, progression and ending, with the session feeling organised and purposeful.
CRegulation & Support
1. Recognises regulation needsIdentifies signs of dysregulation, sensory needs, emotional changes or behavioural changes.
2. Adapts support appropriatelyAdjusts approach, pacing, communication and expectations to match the participant’s needs.
3. Uses strategies to support regulationUses breaks, visuals, reassurance, structure or co regulation strategies to maintain calm, participation and focus.
4. Maintains a consistent and regulated presenceRemains calm, predictable and supportive, helping the participant feel safe and regulated throughout the session.
